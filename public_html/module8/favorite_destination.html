Maranatha Camp

Maranatha Camp is a retreat center that me and my family have been going to for the last 17 years.
The place has everything you need to the point that you could be dropped off there with nothing
but your clothes (and some cash) and be all set.

Here is a list of a few things here.

  • Lake/Pool
  • Sweet Shop
  • Amphitheater
  • Horse back riding
  • Paintball
  • And plenty of other cool things
